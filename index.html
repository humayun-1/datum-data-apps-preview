What are Data Apps?

Applications built on top of business data.

Data Apps are production applications built on top of business data. They turn trusted data into client portals, embedded analytics, internal tools, and workflows that teams and customers can actually use.

Instead of stopping at dashboards or reports, Data Apps make data actionable inside product experiences, operational workflows, and AI-assisted systems.

Frequently asked questions

Short answers for teams deciding whether they need a dashboard, a workflow, or a production data application.

What are Data Apps?

Data Apps are production applications built on top of business data, such as client portals, embedded analytics, data engineering interfaces, workflow applications, and agentic workflows.

Who is this for?

SaaS and data-heavy teams that already have useful data, but need better portals, data engineering interfaces, embedded analytics, or AI workflows on top of it.

Is this dashboard development?

Not only. Dashboards can be part of the build, but the focus is production software, customer experience, access control, workflows, integrations, and usage.

What is embedded analytics?

It means analytics or reporting experiences built inside a product, platform, or client portal, instead of sending reports manually.

What are agentic workflows?

Agentic workflows use LLMs, tools, APIs, business context, approvals, and logs to complete controlled steps inside real operations.

Can you work with our existing data stack?

Yes. Data Apps are designed to sit on top of existing warehouses, databases, APIs, analytics tools, and business systems.

How long does a build take?

Most builds depend on scope, integrations, data readiness, and production requirements. The goal is to build properly, not ship a throwaway demo.

What makes this different from generic full-stack development?

The focus is data-heavy application experiences: access control, analytics, validation flows, integrations, and actions on top of business data.
